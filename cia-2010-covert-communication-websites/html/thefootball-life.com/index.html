Organizations

  • UEFA (Union of European Football Associations)
    Official site of the governing body of football on the continent of Europe, UEFA. Includes UEFA Cup, Champions League, and Euro tournament information, recent news, feature stories, media, team information, and a magazine.
  • Asian Football Confederation (AFC)
    Official site of the confederation to develop and promote football in Asia. Features links to event calendar, competitions, photo gallery, and national associations.
  • AFC Champions League
    Features tournament and event information, fan zone, and latest results.
  • African Confederation of Football (CAF)
    Activities on African football and competitions organized by the African football competitions.
  • Oceania Football Confederation (OFC)
    Official site with news, calendar, competitions, photographs, links, contacts, and publications, which include The Wave.
