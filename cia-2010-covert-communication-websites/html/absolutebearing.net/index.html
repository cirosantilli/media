Absolute Bearing is a prime resource for information relating to the growing market in yachting and sailing. Over the last twenty years there has been an increase in the popularity of large, private luxury yachts. While the Mediterranean and Caribbean Seas rule as private yacht cruising territory, luxury yachts have the ability to cruise all parts of the world. The increase in deamand has also brought an increase in custom yacht manufacturers, yacht brokers, and yacht charters. Europe and the United States remain the predominant leaders in yacht manufacturing however the trend is growning throughout the modern world.

Ranking the World's largest yachts

Luxurious boats and superyachts will continue to get bigger and longer as will their delivery numbers. Germany and the Netherlands are the largest producers of luxury yachts although Italy and the United States are not far behind.
